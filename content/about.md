Hi — I'm **Omar Karim Chtioui**, a cloud engineer based in Italy.

I work at [Klarna](https://www.klarna.com/), where I build and operate the
centralized, AWS-based deployment platform that powers releases for 150+
engineering teams — auto-scaling, high availability, networking, secrets
management, and blue/green and canary promotion workflows, all held to
bank-grade security and compliance standards. Before joining the platform
team I spent a couple of years as a backend engineer on Klarna's payments,
helping ship new payment options as the company expanded into new markets.

Earlier I was a software engineer at IBM, designing distributed,
multi-tiered web applications and building robotic process automation — and
before that I cut my teeth at a smaller company writing REST APIs,
cross-platform desktop apps, and a computer-vision digital-signage product
deployed across 10k+ devices. I studied Computer Engineering at Politecnico
di Bari and hold the AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate
certification.

This site is my personal space on the web: a place to write things down,
think out loud, and keep a record of what I'm working on and learning —
mostly cloud infrastructure and backend engineering, with the occasional
detour into AI and prompting. You'll find essays, technical notes, and the
odd half-formed idea over in the [posts](/posts/) section.
